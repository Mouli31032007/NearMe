Theerthamalai temple, or Shri Theerthagirishwarar Temple, is a historic Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located atop a hill in the Dharmapuri District of Tamil Nadu. It is renowned for its sacred spring and its connection to the epic Ramayana, with a unique legend involving Lord Rama, Hanuman, and a sacred river. The temple is a significant pilgrimage site, especially during the annual festivals and Mahashivarathiri.
